# Step 2: Iterating on Design

The power of Artifacts is in the **iteration**. You don't need to rewrite the code; just tell Claude what to change.

### Sub-prompts to try:
1.  `Change the balls to be neon blue and add a dark mode background.`
2.  `Add a slider to control the gravity of the simulation.`
3.  `Make the balls collide with a satisfying sound effect (mocked).`

### Why this matters:
Traditional development requires manually locating and changing lines of code. With Artifacts, you describe the *desired state*, and the AI handles the implementation.
