# Iterative Refinement

Prompting is an iterative process. If the AI doesn't give you what you want, don't just give up — **refine your prompt**.

### Effective Refinement Techniques:
1.  **Add Constraints**: "Keep the answer under 50 words."
2.  **Define a Persona**: "Explain this like I'm 5." or "Act as a Senior Research Scientist."
3.  **Specify Output Format**: "Provide the response as a JSON object."

### Exercise:
Refine the following prompt to be more specific:
`Tell me about AI.`

**Better Version**:
`Act as an AI Historian. Provide a bulleted list of the top 3 milestones in AI development between 2010 and 2024. Keep each point brief.`
