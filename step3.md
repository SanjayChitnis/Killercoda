# Step 3: Exporting and Sharing

Once you are happy with your Artifact, you can use the code in your own applications.

### How to use the code:
1.  In the Claude Artifact window, click the **"Code"** tab.
2.  Copy the entire block of code.
3.  Paste it into your React/Next.js project.

### Tip for REVA Students:
If you are building a presentation or a project, you can generate **Interactive Quizzes** or **Simulators** and embed them directly.

**Try this prompt**:
`Generate a comprehensive quiz for a first-year biology student on the topic of Cell Mitosis. Make it interactive with progress tracking.`
